86Borders.com

I’ve worked at 86Borders for three and a half years and gained the title Director of User Experience. While there, I’ve gained extensive experience as a:

  • Business Analyst
  • Product Manager
  • Designer
  • Developer
  • Video Editor
  • Strategist

86Borders is a startup company that has three primary focuses.

At its core, 86Borders is a social media inspired communications platform, which has a focus on unifying business processes that span disparate groups. Individual users can set up personal profiles with photos and other personal details; while organizations can set up groups with logos, thumbnails, and banners. The platform hosts business-grade applications that use the relationships between groups and individuals to define the workflow.

The company’s second focus is 86EnRoute, the first product that sprang from the platform. 86EnRoute is a logistics solution which offers a mobile app for workers, an operations dashboard for management, and client notifications/visibility using text messages with hyperlinks to a real-time tracking application (similar to Uber, but without a need to download an app).

Finally, 86Borders has a healthcare focus. These products, of course, focus on healthcare. The piece that I am involved with here, is a translation of 86EnRoute to suit healthcare clients. There are other aspects of healthcare which 86Borders is heavily involved, but of which lack the expertise or the forum to speak here.


sesra.ussquash.com

The Southeastern Squash Racquets Association (SESRA) was founded in the 1980’s. It functions as a non-profit organization to aid the growth of the squash community in the southeast and promote development of junior squash players.

In January 2017 I was invited to join the board as Director of Communications, to revive the website and expand community outreach using email programs and social media. Over this time, we’ve consistently added content to the website and updated the social media profiles to reflect the SESRA brand; club pros and contributors can log onto these profiles to share content.
