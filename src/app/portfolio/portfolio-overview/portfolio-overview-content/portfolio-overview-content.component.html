SESRA
Director of Communications


sesra.ussquash.com

The Southeastern Squash Racquets Association (SESRA) was founded in the 1980’s. It functions as a non-profit organization to aid the growth of the squash community in the southeast and promote development of junior squash players.

In January 2017 I was invited to join the board as Director of Communications, to revive the website and expand community outreach using email programs and social media. Over this time, we’ve consistently added content to the website and updated the social media profiles to reflect the SESRA brand; club pros and contributors can log onto these profiles to share content.
