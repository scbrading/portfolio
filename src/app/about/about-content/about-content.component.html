About

My name is Clark Brading and I’m a technologist. I went to Georgia Tech, graduating as a Biomedical Engineer, and joined a startup technology company called 86Borders shortly thereafter. While at 86Borders, I gained the title of Director of User Experience and have been the product manager for our main platform (a PAAS solution), our marketing pages, and our logistics solution 86EnRoute.

During my time at 86Borders, I’ve advanced our products by taking requirements from our clients and stakeholders and brought them to life through design and work with web and mobile developers (sometimes doing the development myself). Seeing these items through to completion also means arming our sales team with screenshots, prototypes, and demo videos.

Apart from my regular job, I’ve also been a board member of the South Eastern Squash Racquets Association (SESRA) with the role of Director of Communications for the past year. In this role, I’ve been in charge of overseeing our WordPress website, our Constant Contact email outreach newsletters, and our social media profiles.

I’ve made this website to provide a bit of insight into my world of technology. It functions as a comprehensive example of my range of skills, as I design, develop, and support it myself. To see how I made it, check out my GitHub here.
